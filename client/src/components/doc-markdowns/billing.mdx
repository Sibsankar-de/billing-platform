# Billing & Invoices

The Invoices module enables you to construct professional bills, record client transactions, handle payments, and print standard invoices.

---

## Creating a New Invoice (Bill)

To draft a new invoice:
1. Navigate to the **Billing** tab or select **Create Bill** in your dashboard.
2. Complete the billing form sections:
   * **Bill To (Customer)**: Enter the customer's name, phone, and address.
   * **Items Selection**: Select products from your inventory, specify the quantity, and choose the active pricing breakdown.
   * **Discounts & Taxes**: Input absolute discounts or custom tax percentages.
   * **Payment Details**: Enter the paid amount to calculate any outstanding dues. Select a payment mode (Cash, Card, UPI, Bank Transfer).

---

## Customer Auto-Linking

When typing in the **Bill To** input field:
* EaseInv performs a **search-as-you-type** query against your registered customers.
* Selecting an existing customer auto-fills their phone, email, and address, and lists their current outstanding dues.
* If the customer does not exist, type their info manually. EaseInv automatically creates a new customer profile when the invoice is saved!

---

## Saving & Printing Bills

EaseInv provides two modes of storing and printing invoices:

### 1. Saving Bills
* Click **Save** or use the shortcut `Ctrl + S`.
* Invoices are stored in the database. You can save an invoice as a **Draft** or mark it as **Paid** (which updates cash flows).

### 2. Printing Bills
* Click **Save & Print** or use the shortcut `Ctrl + P`.
* A responsive modal displays the print layout. You can select your system printer or save directly as a **PDF**.

## Related Invoice Settings

Customize your billing templates and details under [Store Settings > Invoice Settings](/stores/[store_id]/settings/invoice):

### Invoice Numbering & Rounding
* **Invoice Prefix**: Define a custom prefix (e.g. `INV-`) to generate serial numbering automatically (e.g. `INV-1001`, `INV-1002`).
* **Roundup Invoice Total**: Toggle to round final amounts to the nearest whole integer (e.g., `$45.75` rounds to `$46.00`).

### Branding
* **Store Logo**: Upload a logo to be displayed in the header of the printed invoices.
* **Footer Note**: Customize a message at the bottom of the invoice (e.g., *"Thank you for your business!"*).

### Payments & Banking
* **Payment QR Code**: Upload a UPI or payment QR code so customers can scan and pay directly from printed or digital invoices.
* **Bank Account Details**: Provide bank details (Account Name, Bank Name, Account Number, IFSC/Bank Code) printed on invoices for direct bank transfers.
