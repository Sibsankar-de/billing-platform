# Introduction to EaseInv

Welcome to **EaseInv**, a streamlined, developer-first billing, invoicing, and inventory management platform built for modern businesses. Whether you are running a single local store or managing complex business operations across multiple branches, EaseInv provides the exact tools you need to stay organized, track inventory, and handle customer transactions efficiently.

---

## Core Features

EaseInv brings all of your business management workflows into one centralized workspace:

### 1. Unified Dashboard
Get a high-level view of your business health. Access real-time analytics, visual revenue graphs, total invoices, active customer counts, and alerts for low-stock items.

### 2. Smart Invoicing
Generate clean, professional invoices in seconds.
* Print invoices or save them as PDFs.
* Customize invoice numbers, notes, tax rates, and discount settings.
* Manage multiple payment modes (Cash, Card, UPI, Bank Transfer).

### 3. Real-Time Inventory & Product Tracking
Keep control of your catalog with our powerful inventory tools:
* Tracks product stock level and issues low-stock warnings.
* Categorize items and support multiple currency symbols.
* Search and filter products instantly.

### 4. Customer Relationship Management
Build stronger relationships with your customers:
* View total purchases, outstanding balances, and historical invoices for each customer.
* Keep customer profiles updated with emails, phones, and addresses.

### 5. API-First Architecture
Integrate EaseInv directly with your custom apps, websites, or ERP systems using our Developer API. Check out our [Authentication Guide](/docs/authentication) and [API Reference](/docs/api) to get started!

---

## Getting Started in 3 Steps

Follow this quick guide to configure your EaseInv workspace:

1. **Create your Store**: Navigate to the Stores section in your dashboard and click "Create Store". Define your store name, currency settings, and physical address.
2. **Add Products**: Navigate to the Inventory tab and click "Add Product". Input the product name, SKU, buying price, selling price, and initial stock.
3. **Draft your First Invoice**: Go to the Invoices tab, click "Create Invoice", select a customer or add a new one, select your products, and click "Submit".
