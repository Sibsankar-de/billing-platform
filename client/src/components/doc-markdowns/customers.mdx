# Customer Management

The Customer module in EaseInv provides a comprehensive directory to build client relationships, manage client accounts, track outstanding due balances, and view customer purchase history.

---

## The Customer Directory

Navigate to the **Customers** tab to view your client ledger. For each customer, the directory displays:
* **Name & Contact**: Phone number, email, and billing address.
* **Outstanding Dues**: Total unpaid balances across all invoices.
* **Purchase Statistics**: Total number of invoices generated and total lifetime spending.

---

## Managing Customer Profiles

EaseInv makes it easy to add and update profiles:

### 1. Creating a Customer
1. Click the **Create Customer** button.
2. Provide the following details:
   * **Name**: The full name of the client (required).
   * **Phone Number**: Contact number for billing updates.
   * **Email**: Email address for invoices.
   * **Address**: Main billing/shipping address.
3. Click **Create Customer** to save.

> [!TIP]
> You can also create customers on-the-fly during checkout. Simply type their name and contact info in the billing form, and saving the invoice will automatically create their profile in your directory!

### 2. Updating a Customer
1. Click the **Edit** (pencil) icon next to a customer row.
2. Modify contact or address fields in the modal.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

### 3. Deleting a Customer
1. Click the **Delete** (trash) icon on a customer row.
2. Confirm the action in the prompt. 

> [!WARNING]
> Deleting a customer will remove their contact profile but does not delete their historical billing transactions, ensuring your accounting records remain accurate.

---

## Transaction History

Clicking on any customer row opens their **Customer Details View**:
* **Purchase History**: A timeline of all bills drafted or paid for this customer.
* **Due Summary**: Highlights unpaid balances with direct links to settle or print the invoices.
